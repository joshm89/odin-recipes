Lasagne

Description

Lasagne is a beloved Italian dish renowned for its rich, comforting layers of flavors and textures. It features tender lasagne noodles, which serve as the foundation for alternating layers of savory meat sauce and creamy cheese filling. The meat sauce, often made with ground beef or Italian sausage, is simmered with tomatoes, garlic, onions, and a blend of aromatic herbs, creating a robust and hearty flavor profile. The cheese filling, typically a mixture of ricotta cheese, Parmesan, and eggs, adds a creamy and slightly tangy contrast to the meat sauce, enhanced with freshly chopped parsley for a burst of freshness.

The lasagne is then crowned with a generous topping of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese, which melt and turn golden brown during baking, forming a deliciously gooey and slightly crispy top layer. When baked to perfection, the dish showcases beautifully distinct layers that meld together to create a harmonious blend of tastes and textures. The tender pasta, rich meat sauce, and creamy cheese filling make lasagne a quintessential comfort food that is both satisfying and indulgent, perfect for family gatherings and special occasions.

Ingredients

  • Lasagne noodles
  • 450 grams ground beef or Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, diced
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 1 can (800 grams) crushed tomatoes
  • 2 tablespoons (30 grams) tomato paste
  • 1 teaspoon dried oregano
  • 1 teaspoon dried basil
  • 1/2 teaspoon dried thyme
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 500 grams ricotta cheese
  • 50 grams grated Parmesan cheese
  • 2 eggs, beaten
  • 2 tablespoons fresh parsley, chopped
  • 300 grams shredded mozzarella cheese

Steps

  1. Preheat your oven to 190°C (375°F).
  2. Cook the lasagne noodles according to the package instructions, then drain and set aside.
  3. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef or Italian sausage over medium heat until browned. Add the diced onion and minced garlic, and cook until the onion is translucent.
  4. Stir in the crushed tomatoes, tomato paste, dried oregano, dried basil, dried thyme, salt, and pepper. Simmer the sauce for about 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  5. In a mixing bowl, combine the ricotta cheese, grated Parmesan cheese, beaten eggs, and chopped parsley. Mix until well blended.
  6. Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce at the bottom of a baking dish.
  7. Place a layer of lasagne noodles over the sauce.
  8. Spread a layer of the ricotta cheese mixture over the noodles.
  9. Top with another layer of meat sauce and sprinkle with shredded mozzarella cheese.
  10. Repeat the layers (noodles, ricotta mixture, meat sauce, mozzarella) until all the ingredients are used, ending with a final layer of meat sauce and a generous topping of mozzarella and Parmesan cheese.
  11. Cover the baking dish with aluminum foil and bake in the preheated oven for 25 minutes.
  12. Remove the foil and bake for an additional 25 minutes, or until the cheese is golden and bubbly.
  13. Let the lasagne rest for 10-15 minutes before serving to allow the layers to set.
